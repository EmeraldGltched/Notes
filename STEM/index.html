  • X-Acto Knife Safety!

    • You will have a quiz over the information tomorrow.
    • You must get 100% on the quiz in order to use an x-acto knife in this class.
      • X-ACTO knives are small and can hurt anyone.
      • It is never ok to horseplay when the knives are out for use.
      • Anyone horseplaying will have a referal written and sent to the office.
      • You need a cutting board anytime you use an X-ACTO knife.
      • Always be aware of your surroundings.
      • Hold the X-ACTO knife like a pencil.
      • Wear a glove on the hand that is not holding the knife.
      • Keep fingers away from the blade.
